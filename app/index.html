About

Hi, call me Dan! I am currently a Senior Software Engineer at Enverus working on mostly frontend React applications. I have over 10 years of experience developing web applications and working on different parts of the stack. I consider myself a full-stack developer, but recently I have enjoyed focusing on frontend work and security. I want to solve problems and build applications with a security-oriented mindset.

To better understand how computers and the internet work through the lens of security, I am earning a Master of Science in Cybersecurity from Syracuse University. In earning an MS in Cybersecurity, I have revealed a curiosity in the hardware-software interface that I hope to grow and expand upon. I have a Bachelor of Science in Geophysical Engineering, with a minor in Computational and Applied Mathematics, and an area of special interest in Space and Planetary Science and Engineering. This background has enabled me to gain skills in web development, data visualization, privacy, security, , GIS (Geographic Information Systems), and remote sensing.

I believe that Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion are crucial to a successful business and way of living life. Every day I strive to live a more diverse life and be more inclusive. I have been a member of the DEI committee at Enverus for the past two years where we have worked on improving diversity in hiring, implementing mentorship programs, and celebrating diversity within our organization. For any role I take in the future, it is important to me that we can work to improve or implement DEI initiatives.
